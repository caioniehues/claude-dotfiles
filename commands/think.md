# Think - Simple Structured Thinking

<task>
Apply basic structured thinking when needed: $ARGUMENTS
</task>

## When to use
- Complex problems requiring analysis
- Multiple solution approaches to evaluate
- Need to break down requirements

## Simple structure
1. **Problem**: What needs solving?
2. **Options**: What are the approaches?
3. **Decision**: Which approach and why?
4. **Action**: What to do next?

No elaborate thinking orchestration - just clear reasoning when complexity demands it.
